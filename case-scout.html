Scout Motors
Retail Rollout

Role
Senior Design Lead
Scope
Prototype + National Rollout
Focus
Scalability, Brand Experience

Built a scalable retail system translating a new automotive brand into a physical experience—balancing storytelling, operational efficiency, and rapid deployment.

The Challenge

Launch a new automotive retail concept from zero. The system needed to communicate brand identity while supporting rapid multi-market rollout under tight timelines and evolving product definition.

Approach

Developed a modular design framework allowing core brand elements to remain consistent while adapting to diverse site conditions. Prioritized flexible planning grids, repeatable fixture systems, and material strategies that scale.

Integrated customer journey mapping into spatial planning—aligning digital touchpoints, product display, and storytelling zones to create a cohesive experience.

System Thinking

Established a prototype kit-of-parts approach enabling faster rollout cycles. Standardized components reduced design iteration time and improved coordination across architecture, fabrication, and construction teams.

Impact

• Enabled consistent brand execution across multiple markets

• Reduced build timelines through modular design

• Created a scalable framework for future expansion

• Aligned cross-functional teams through clear design system
